Why the Website?

Summit of Camels Hump VT, Summer 2011

Welcome! As an indomitable geek, I (Jeremy Audet) have staked out a small spot on the web. I built this site to learn more about web technology. I use the web almost every single day (I've verified this statement using Google history), and a healthy curiosity about the medium has led me here. How do those nifty navigation bars come into being? Now I know.

In Brief

As my dad would say, I am a social solitary person. I'm happy to play a game of racquetball, frisbee, or magic with you, or even spend the rare afternoon at the beach. However, you're more likely to find me biking, reading, or learning about computer technology.

As my work experience shows, I'm a computer geek. I've worked my way up from the bottom of the ladder, doing everything from acting as a sales representative at Best Buy to engineering a satellite for Vermont Technical College. You can learn more by glancing at my resume or otherwise browsing this site.
