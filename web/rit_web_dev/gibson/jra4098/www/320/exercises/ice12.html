Dear Linux nuts:

I think it's pretty damn cool that you have the interest you do. In fact, I think it's so cool that I'm gonna throw a real curveball your way.

  1. Below is an image of Tux the penguin. Save a copy on your computer (say, in your Downloads folder).
  2. Turn down the volume on your speakers. Don't mute them, but turn them down.
  3. Open up a terminal (command line window).
  4. Try the following commands. One of them should work on your computer. If you saved the image in your downloads folder, "/path/to/image" will likely be "~/Downloads/tux.png".
    • aplay /path/to/image
    • cat /path/to/image > /dev/dsp
    • cat /path/to/image > /dev/sound/dsp
  5. Be amazed.
