About Mike Audet

Mike did his undergraduate work at University of Vermont and Burlington College, studying Geology, European History, and Counseling Psychology. He earned his MA in Counseling Psychology at the Antioch New England Graduate School in Keene, NH, graduating Summa Cum Laude.

Mike has many years of classroom teaching experience as an adjunct professor of Psychology, Health Sciences, and Counseling. He is also a Licensed Clinical Mental Health Counselor and a Certified Master Tutor, having earned his tutoring certificate at Johnson State College.

About the Clinic

When you arrive at Back to Basics Reading Clinic, drive up the driveway and turn right into the parking lot. Come through the gate and up the steps to enter the clinic. You will be greeted by one labrador retriever, colored brown. He loves children and children love him! Please let us know if you have allergies.
